ABOUT US

Gandra Fincorp Private Limited represents a next-generation Non-Banking Financial Company (NBFC) strategically positioned at the intersection of regulatory excellence, institutional capital deployment, and technological innovation. With RBI licensing secured and global advisory partnerships with PricewaterhouseCoopers, the company exemplifies sophisticated financial architecture designed for multi-jurisdictional competitiveness while maintaining absolute regulatory compliance.

VISION

We aspire to achieve excellence in our industry as the leader with strong international presence by providing practical strategies that help our company's clients meet their challenges. Our objective is consistent, high-quality, specialized service delivered on a global basis in each region where commodity trading companies operate.

MISSION

Our mission is to provide superior service and continuously increase our market share in the commodity products arena. The company's objective is to provide high-quality, specialized service delivered to clients.

OUR COMMITMENT

Our understanding of the demands and challenges trading companies face enables us to develop services and methodologies that specifically address the needs of this sector. We look at industry challenges from multiple angles, pooling our knowledge and resources together to accomplish to fit our client's ever-changing requirements.

OUR TEAM

We have a confident management team that share responsibilities for the supervision of the company, deployment of its strategy, and to achieve its objectives. The team has subject matter expertise including management, procurement, logistics and sales skills required to successfully market the products with efficiency. Our global network comprises of businesses, tradespersons and professionals in the same or related fields, enabling us to pool resources, share information and identify best practices that would benefit our clients.
